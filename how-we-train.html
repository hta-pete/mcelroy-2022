7 WEEK DRIVER
TRAINING SCHEDULE:

  • Week 1:
    Orientation
  • Week 2:
    On-the-yard training at McElroy. Work with safety managers. Learn skills like load securement, backing & DOT regulations. Company provided hotel, breakfast and lunch during orientation & yard training.
  • Weeks 3-6:
    On-the-road training with driver trainer.
  • Week 7:
    Final week training. Skills testing & evaluation. Truck assigned.

HIRING CRITERIA:

  • Min. 21 years old
  • Class A CDL
  • Able to lift/carry 100-lb. tarps
  • Able to climb
  • Solid work history
  • Pass urine & hair follicle drug screen
  • No DUI, DWI in the past 5 years
  • No reckless or careless driving in the past 5 years
  • No alcohol or drug conviction in the past 5 years
